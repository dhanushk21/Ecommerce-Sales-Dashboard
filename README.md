📊 Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard
A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing Year-To-Date (YTD) performance across sales, profit, and quantity metrics. This project provides deep dives into product performance and regional distribution to drive data-backed business decisions.

🎯 Key Insights & Features

📈 Executive Summary KPI Cards: Real-time tracking of $11.53M in YTD Sales and $1.34M in YTD Profit, including a 11.6% Profit Margin.

📅 Year-Over-Year (YoY) Category Analysis: A detailed breakdown of Office Supplies, Technology, and Furniture, highlighting that Furniture is the only category showing positive YoY growth of +0.73%.

🌍 Geographic Sales Distribution: An interactive map and donut chart showing the West region leading with 32.2% of total sales.

🏆 Product Performance Benchmarking:

🔝 Top 5 Products: Identified Staple envelope as the highest contributor at $57K.

⬇️ Bottom 5 Products: Pinpointed low-performing items like the Rediform S.O.S. Phone to identify areas for inventory optimization.

🚢 Shipping & Segmentation: Analysis showing that Standard Class is the preferred shipping method, accounting for 60.5% of all orders.

🛠️ Technical Stack

🖥️ Tool: Power BI Desktop.

🏗️ Data Modeling: Star Schema with dedicated Measure tables.

🧪 DAX Functions: Utilized for YTD calculations, YoY growth percentages, and dynamic formatting.

🎨 Visualizations: Custom-themed area charts, donut charts, and map-based spatial analysis.
